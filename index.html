My Story

I got my first paycheck from the local grocery store in Sioux Falls at 15, and I spent nearly all of it on a welder. Since then, I have gained valuable insights into what makes things tick by studying engineering and design at Northwestern.

I have found success patenting the Polar Bench, developing pilot controls for Boeing, and researching solar chargers in Germany.
